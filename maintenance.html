Pool Maintenance & Equipment

Professional Care & Repair Services

Why Regular Maintenance Matters

Your swimming pool is a significant investment. Regular maintenance protects that investment by keeping water safe to swim in, extending equipment lifespan, preventing costly repairs, and ensuring your pool remains crystal clear year-round. Professional care catches small issues before they become expensive problems — saving you money and headaches in the long run.

Our Maintenance Services

Pool Cleaning & Vacuuming

Weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly cleaning. Includes skimming, brushing walls, and vacuuming the bottom to keep your pool pristine.

Water Chemistry Balancing

Professional testing and chemical balancing. We maintain proper pH, alkalinity, chlorine, and calcium levels for safe, comfortable swimming.

Filter Cleaning & Backwashing

Regular filter maintenance extends equipment life and improves water clarity. We clean cartridge filters and perform backwashing as needed.

Pump & Motor Repair

Diagnosing and repairing pump and motor issues. We handle leaks, performance problems, and equipment replacement if needed.

Heater Service & Repair

Gas and electric heater maintenance, troubleshooting, and repair. Keep your pool warm and comfortable year-round.

Salt System Maintenance

Salt chlorinator cell cleaning, calcium buildup removal, and system troubleshooting. Keep your salt system running efficiently.

Winterization & Pool Closing

Professional seasonal closing. We drain, clean, and protect your pool for winter with proper chemical treatment and equipment winterization.

Spring Opening

Get your pool ready for summer. We open, fill, clean, balance chemistry, and inspect all equipment for peak performance.

Equipment Replacement

Upgrade or replace worn-out pumps, filters, lights, controllers, and other components. We handle installation and startup.

Leak Detection & Repair

Detect water leaks in plumbing, liners, or equipment. We locate the problem and provide repair solutions to prevent water waste.
