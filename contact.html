Common Questions

How much does a pool cost?

Pool pricing varies by type (gunite, fiberglass, vinyl) and size. Vinyl pools start at $18,500. We provide free quotes based on your specific needs.

How long does installation take?

Vinyl & fiberglass: 3–6 weeks. Gunite: 6–8 weeks depending on complexity and weather. We'll give you a timeline with your quote.

Do you offer financing?

Yes! We work with several financing partners. Contact us for details on payment plans and interest rates.

What's your service area?

We serve Oklahoma City and surrounding areas, with travel available up to 200 miles. Contact us to confirm your location.

Do you provide maintenance?

Absolutely. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly maintenance plans. We also provide seasonal opening & closing services.

What warranty do you offer?

1-year workmanship warranty included. Extended warranties available. Details provided with your quote.
